Meet Dr. Maria Jimenez, DDS

Dr. Maria Jimenez began her dental education in the Dominican Republic and graduated with her dentistry degree in 1990. After moving to the United States, she continued her dental education at New York University College of Dentistry, a global leader and pioneering research center. Graduating in 1998 with her DDS Degree. She furthered her education in orthodontic treatment with two years of study at Progressive Orthodontic Seminars.

Dentistry is a rapidly developing field which means that Dr. Jimenez continues her education every year to keep up with the latest developments. To ensure that her patients receive the very best in care, Dr. Jimenez ensures that her staff also update their training regularly.

We welcome new patients and those seeking second opinions on their care. Of course, we are bilingual, speaking both English and Spanish. Dr. Jimenez is proud to serve the families of New Haven as well as students at nearby Yale University and the University of New Haven. We appreciate every patient who trusts us with their health. We strive to maintain the highest standards of care, excellence in our technical skill and keeping abreast of the best dental science technology for our patients’ dental health.

But even with this emphasis on excellence, we still feel like our patients are our family and we strive to create a warm, welcoming environment for each person, whether they are new or we have been greeting them for years. Especially for children or people who are fearful about visiting the dentist, we find that a friendly atmosphere actually makes it easier for a person to take good care of their teeth and oral health.

By investing in continual expansion of our skills, we are able to offer preventative care; restorative care including fillings, implants, simple and complex extractions; root canal therapy; veneers, crowns and bridges; cosmetic care and orthodontic treatment; all under one roof. You won’t need to run all over town to care for your teeth—in this one location, we may be able to offer every service you or your family will ever need. Protect both the beauty of your teeth and your overall health by visiting Albania Dental for the best and most friendly dental care in New Haven.
